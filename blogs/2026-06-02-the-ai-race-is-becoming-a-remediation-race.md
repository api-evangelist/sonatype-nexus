As AI accelerates vulnerability discovery, the bottleneck shifts from finding security flaws to fixing them, requiring coordination across the entire software supply chain including maintainers, registries, distros, and package managers. Brian Fox argues that effective remediation must ensure repairs accumulate in public repositories rather than dispersing into private patches, so that the shared source of truth is preserved. The goal is a repair system that is fast enough for consumers, credible enough for security teams, and open enough to preserve the ecosystem.
